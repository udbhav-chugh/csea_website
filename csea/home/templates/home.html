What is CSEA??

The Computer Science and Engineering Association(CSEA) is a student body
under the guidance of Professors which works for the welfare of students of CSE department and bridge the gap between students of different years.

Domains

Industrial Preparation

Coding skills are now essential part of professional and research domains. CSEA conducts events such as Coding Competitions and Hackathons to encourage students to hone their coding skills. Various workshops on web development, network management, understanding our operating system etc. are conducted by CSEA to give students a taste of development
